Dabs is an online retailer that was founded in Bolton in 1987 by David Atherton and Bruce Smith from which the name of the company arrives. It specialises in selling hardware, software as well as other computer accessories which it originally sold through mail order but now uses internet retail and is one of the biggest online retailers of electronics in the Republic of Ireland.
